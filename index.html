What We Built
Built For The Field

From the boss's dashboard to the worker's phone — OilManager keeps your whole crew connected and accountable.

📍

Job Dispatch & Map Pins

Create a job, drop a pin on the lease map, assign your crew. Workers see it on their phone instantly — no calls, no confusion.

⏱️

Digital Time Cards

Workers clock in and out from their phone. GPS verifies they're on-site. No more paper cards, no more guessing hours.

🗺️

Lease Road Navigator

Custom overlays on Google Maps for caliche and private lease roads. Search by lease name and get turn-by-turn directions.

📊

Boss Dashboard

Bird's-eye view of every worker and job on one screen. See who's clocked in, who's idle, and what jobs need attention right now.

📱

SMS Job Alerts

New job assigned? Workers get a text immediately — even on a dead-zone lease where data won't load the app.

📄

Payroll Export

Approve time cards from the dashboard and export a clean PDF or CSV for payroll. Cuts hours out of your admin week.

Simple Process
Up and Running
in 10 Minutes

No training required. If your crew can text, they can use OilManager.

1

Boss Sets Up the Company

Create your account, add your workers by phone number, and set up your lease locations on the map.

2

Create & Assign Jobs

Drop a pin, write the job details, pick your crew. Workers get a text and see it in the app.

3

Crew Works — You Track It Live

Workers clock in on-site via GPS. You watch status updates from the boss dashboard in real time.

4

Approve Hours & Export

Review and approve time cards at the end of the week. Export to PDF and hand it to payroll. Done.
