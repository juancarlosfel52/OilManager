We Strongly Recommend Using a Company / Work Phone

This feature sends SMS messages from your registered number to field workers. OilManager does not store, sell, or share your personal phone number — it is saved only on your own server. However, to protect your privacy, we strongly recommend using a dedicated work or company phone number only. Never enter a personal cell number. Your number will never be visible to workers — only to you as the sender.

Twilio Setup
1
Go to twilio.com/try-twilio and create a free account.
2
From your Twilio Console, copy your Account SID and Auth Token.
3
Buy a Twilio phone number (≈$1/mo). This is what workers will see as the sender.
4
Open .env in your OilManager folder and paste your TWILIO_ACCOUNT_SID, TWILIO_AUTH_TOKEN, and TWILIO_PHONE_NUMBER.
5
Run npm install then node server.js (or use start.bat).
6
Add worker phone numbers in data/workers.json or from the dashboard.
